Start ContractFix Now.

ContractFix is a cloud-based contract negotiation and management platform
that allows for effortless contract writing and signing.

Spend less time chasing emails

With ContractFix, you can spend your time perfecting the contract instead of figuring out if the other party received what version of contract, and when. Simply invite the other signing party through email and both parties can view, edit, and sign contracts. ContractFix also helps you see all of your past stored contracts so you can keep track of everything you signed.

Work together, faster and better

You can start writing from an empty editor or choose from a list of ContractFix's templates. We have contract templates ranging from rentals to household services and add comments to contracts and track the history of changes made to each contract.

ContractFix is easy to use for everybody

ContractFix is designed to cater to individual needs. Making an account with and using ContractFix is intuitive and digital signatures make signing quick and easy while providing all levels of security. YOu can also download finalized contracts in PDFs.

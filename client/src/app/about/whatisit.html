What is ContractFix?



OUR STORY

Most of us can agree that verbal agreements are great if they are properly kept! However, that is not always the case. For example, many of us have experiences renting a place through Airbnb but then ending up with surprise fees on top of the rent fee because specifics of the rental were not put into a binding contract. Ironically, we now live in a world where majority of negotiations or agreements are made online but there is no easy enough system that allows contract writing, editing, and signing for individuals. ContractFix was born out of this frustration.

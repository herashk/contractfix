How Does ContractFix Work?

IT IS EASY TO USE CONTRACTFIX


1.
Simply make an account with ContractFix and verify your email account by clicking on the link sent by us.
2.
Log in to your account. From the dashboard, you can select a contract from the template library and begin customizing it. If you wish, you can write your own by selecting an empty editor.
3.
Click to edit, save, and review changes. You can also click to share the contract with a collaborator by entering in the other party's email address. Both parties can view and edit the contract.
4.
Once the contract is completed, click to sign it. Upon signing, you can no longer make changes to the contract.
5.
You can always view your past stored contracts in the dashboard and can download them in PDF as well.
